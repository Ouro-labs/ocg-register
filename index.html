Developer quickstart

Attach a real app through the production-safe path.

Generate a buildable sample, lint the manifest, validate before business logic, then show consent and a signed receipt. The public registry surface is live for signed discovery and reviewed submissions.

1Buildable Android/iOS samplesmeshkit init target python3 -m meshkit_ocg.cli init target --platform android --app-id com.example.notes --capability notes.append_note --risk write:user_content --output app
2Manifest lintmeshkit manifest lint python3 -m meshkit_ocg.cli manifest lint app/ocg-manifest.json
3Doctor checksmeshkit doctor android python3 -m meshkit_ocg.cli doctor android --project app
4Consent sheet

Target app approval UI appears before risky side effects.

5Receipt view

Caller sees a target-owned signed receipt after completion.
